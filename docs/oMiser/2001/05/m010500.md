Apart from the
[MOb directory](https://orcmid.github.io/miser/oMiser/2011/04/m110400.html)
static class and its operation, the only oMiser classes are those of
dynamically-created ob-cells and their Iob COM interfaces.

Initial ob-cells for construction and operation on ob structures are obtained
via the MOb directory IMob interfaces and operations such as those of the
[friendly API](https://orcmid.github.io/miser/oMiser/2011/04/m110400c.html)
operations `omGetPrimitive` and `omGetLindy`.  All further composition is
conducted using the delivered Iob interfaces, with eventual retention by the
`omSetBinding` operation and subsequent retrieval of constructed obs via the
`omGetBinding` MOb operation for additional construction and script usage.
