  2025-10-24 Look into pseudo-random generators as ways to generate hashes from other hashes.  This would have the HashFlag be treated in some manner as (part of) the current state of an RNG with significant cyclicity.   One of the diehard RNGs that have easy next-random procedures might work.  GB_FLIP is not suitable in this situation.  Also check Knuth on this.  This will influence how big we want the HashFlag to be and how we place the Flag bits.
